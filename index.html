the orb


an experiment by Spencer McTavish

From nothingness, comes existence.

You.

You exist in nothingness. You were the nothingness. And now you are a something - a container. An awareness.

Alone in the void.

But the void can create. You came from the void, after all. The void is all around you, pressing in, pulling at the boundaries that make up you. So the void makes something new, inside of you -

Terror.

There is nothing but you.

There is no one, but you.

You are alone.

You will forever be alone.

Inside of you, eternity races across an un-ending plane of nothingness, seeking a release from the agony of an existence made only to be pointless, to suffer, to reflect in upon itself in a spiral of suffering.
The void worms its way inside of you, seeking to claim that which

The spiral snags. Twists.

The void inside of you gives way. The void before you gives way.

A new existence.

Ponder the orb.
Begin again... The creator of creation...
